About Us

Wester Auction & Realty, established in 1989, is a locally owned auction and real estate company specializing in the liquidation of business assets, municipal surplus and real estate. Our staff of ten auctioneers and ten support personnel stand ready to assist you with your liquidation needs, whether at our company warehouse or at your location.

All auctions are promoted through the print media and this Web site.

Who do we work for?

  • Federal, state and local government
  • Hospitality & food service industries
  • Retail sales corporations
  • Industrial & electronic manufacturers
  • Attorneys, banks and estate administrators

What We Sell

  • Real estate
  • Restaurant equipment
  • Commercial industrial & municipal surplus
  • Vehicles & heavy equipment
